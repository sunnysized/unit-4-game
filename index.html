SNOWFALL FOR THE SOPHISTICATED SOIREE

Hidden within each snowflake is a value which will only be revealed by the click of your mouse. This value will be added to your snowglobe, housing all of your snowflakes. Your goal is to match the number of snowflakes in your snowglobe to the predetermined goal in order to create a crystal snowflake soiree! However, too many snowflakes and you'll create a blizzard and lose! Good luck!
